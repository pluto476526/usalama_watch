Emergency Response Guide

Know what to do in different emergency situations

Medical Emergency
  • Call 120 for ambulance
  • Check breathing and pulse
  • Do not move injured person
  • Keep patient warm and comfortable
Fire Emergency
  • Call 999 immediately
  • Evacuate the building
  • Use fire extinguisher if safe
  • Never use elevator during fire
Crime in Progress
  • Call 999 for police
  • Do not confront criminals
  • Note descriptions and details
  • Move to safe location
Road Accident
  • Call 999 and 120
  • Set up warning triangles
  • Do not move injured people
  • Exchange information with others
