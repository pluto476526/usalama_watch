Understanding the Usalama Watch Community Safety Platform

Community Safety

Platform Overview

Usalama Watch is a comprehensive community safety platform that empowers Kenyan citizens to report, track, and stay informed about safety incidents in their neighborhoods. Our system combines modern technology with community collaboration to create safer environments.

The platform operates through multiple channels including web, mobile, and USSD to ensure accessibility for all Kenyans, regardless of their technology access or location.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about how Usalama Watch works

Is Usalama Watch free to use?

Yes, Usalama Watch is completely free for all community members. We believe safety should be accessible to everyone.

Do I need a smartphone to use the service?

No, you can access all essential features through our USSD service (*384*87#) from any mobile phone.

How is my privacy protected?

We offer anonymous reporting, encrypt all data, and comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act. We never sell your data.

What types of incidents should I report?

Report any safety concerns: crimes, accidents, hazards, suspicious activities, or police checkpoints.
